What are UFOs?

In this project, we explore reports of UFO findings in the United States dating back to 1949. Unidentified flying objects (UFOs or flying saucer) are any aerial objects or optical phenomenons not readily identifiable to the observer. UFOs became a major subject of interest following the development of rocketry after World War II and were thought to be intelligent extraterrestrial life vising Earth.

Unidentified flying objects (UFOs or flying saucer) are any aerial objects or optical phenomenons not readily identifiable to the observer. UFOs became a major subject of interest following the development of rocketry after World War II and were thought to be intelligent extraterrestrial life vising Earth.

Studies have shown that the majority of UFO observations are misidentified conventional objects or natural phenomena, however, after further research we are not sure that this is the truth. Although many UFO observations are identified as aircrafts, balloons/sky lanterns, planets, meteors or bright stars, fewer than 10% of reported sightings remain unexplained! This suggests that we cannot rule out proponents of the "extraterrestrial hypothesis" that life outside of Earth is occupying these UFOs.
